Our Story

Rooted in Care, Grown Through Expertise

AgroBerry began as a boutique plant studio with a simple belief: green spaces should feel designed, not decorated. Today we bring that same precision to interior plantscaping, landscape design, and the agricultural supervision of strawberry farms and greenhouses.

Every project starts with observation — of light, soil, climate, and the way people move through a space — before a single plant is chosen. It's this scientific approach, paired with a designer's eye, that sets AgroBerry apart.

Mission

To bring scientifically sound, beautifully designed green solutions to homes, businesses, and farms alike.

Vision

To be the region's most trusted name in landscape design and agricultural consultancy.

Values

Precision, craftsmanship, and an honest respect for how living things actually grow.

How We Work

A Process Built for Results

Every AgroBerry project — large or small — follows the same disciplined seven-step process.

1

Inspection

On-site assessment of light, soil, climate, and existing plant health.

2

Analysis

Lab-informed soil and plant analysis to identify needs and risks.

3

Planning

A tailored plan covering species selection, layout, and timeline.

4

Design

Detailed design concepts — from planting layouts to display styling.

5

Implementation

Careful installation or planting, executed by our trained team.

6

Maintenance

Ongoing care programs to protect your investment long-term.

7

Follow-Up

Scheduled check-ins to adjust care as seasons and conditions change.
